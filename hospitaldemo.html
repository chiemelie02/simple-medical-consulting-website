List of the top rated hospitals in Nigeria.

Nigeria has the 42nd highest population density in the world. The healthcare system in the country is highly fragmented. The government now can provide healthcare for all, which is great news for Nigerians. However, not everyone can afford to pay for healthcare, which is why there are hospitals that cater to the poor. These hospitals are often called “government hospitals”, but many of them are privately owned. While most of us know that Nigeria has hospitals, clinics, and health centers with advanced medical equipment and facilities, most Nigerians don’t know of the best hospitals in Nigeria.
