List of the top rated hospitals in Nigeria.

Nigeria has the 42nd highest population density in the world. The healthcare system in the country is highly fragmented. The government now can provide healthcare for all, which is great news for Nigerians. However, not everyone can afford to pay for healthcare, which is why there are hospitals that cater to the poor. These hospitals are often called “government hospitals”, but many of them are privately owned. While most of us know that Nigeria has hospitals, clinics, and health centers with advanced medical equipment and facilities, most Nigerians don’t know of the best hospitals in Nigeria. We have therefore decided to make the list below of the Top rated Hospitals in Nigeria by analyzing the number of beds, the technology available, the quality of medical services, and the sources of referrals.

1. University College Hospital (UCH)

Ibadan, Nigeria.

The University College Hospital (UCH) Ibadan is a public health institution located in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. The Hospital was established in 1957 as a teaching hospital with a capacity of 1000 beds. This hospital operates for teaching and research purposes. University College Hospital is a government-owned hospital.

  • Address: Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria

  • Beds: 1000

  • Specialty: Research, Teaching hospital

  • Website: www.uch-ibadan.org.ng
